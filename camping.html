RV CAMPING

Come for the scenic views
and the fishing.

Stay for the peace of mind.

One of the best kept secrets about Break Marina is that it’s just close enough to Oakley and Highway 4 to be accessible, and just far enough off the beaten track to be a perfect place to get away from it all. With the new Big Break Marina, we’ve created a clean, safe, family-friendly camping environment while preserving the rustic charm of a historic getaway destination.

CAMPING AMENITIES

Newly installed water hook-ups for RVs
Newly installed electric hook-ups for RVs (110/220v | 30 amps)
Picnic Area
Permanent Restrooms
