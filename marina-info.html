MARINA INFORMATION

THERE'S A LOT TO LIKE
AT THE NEW BIG BREAK MARINA.

Our marina is your marina.

Just off Highway 4, Big Break Marina is your easily accessible gateway to the West Delta. With waters rich with striper, sturgeon, black bass, catfish, bluegill and panfish, freshly renovated launch ramp, an onsite snack shop and a bait & tackle shop just down the street, we’ve got everything you need for a perfect day on the water. If water sports are your thing, Kayak City right inside the marina has got you covered. Or bring your family out for a picnic - dogs welcome! So come on out - the friendly staff here is waiting for you. Grab your perfect day and be a part of the new Big Break Marina.
