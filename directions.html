DIRECTIONS & MAP

GETTING HERE IS EASY.

Getting to Big Break Marina is easy! Follow the directions below to get to the freshly paved Big Break Road. Just be sure you continue PAST the park entrance of our friendly neighbor Big Break Regional Shoreline. You can’t miss us - we’re at the end of the road when you reach the water.

OUR FRIENDLY NEIGHBOR

Big Break Regional Shoreline

This is the entrance to the Big Break Regional Shoreline. Don't stop here, go a bit farther!

OUR ENTRANCE

Enter Big Break Marina Here

Map & Directions

Coming from San Francisco or Oakland

Head East on Highway 4. Take Exit 30 - Hwy 160 Rio Vista/Sacramento
Take Exit 1A - Main Street / E18th St Oakley
Turn Right onto Main Street for 1.1 miles
Turn Left onto Big Break Rd
Continue to the end of Big Break Road until you reach the water.



Coming from Brentwood or Livermore

Head West on Highway 4
Take Exit 30 - Hwy 160 Rio Vista/Sacramento
Take Exit 1A - Main Street / E18th St Oakley
Turn Right onto Main Street for 1.1 miles
Turn Left onto Big Break Rd
Continue to the end of Big Break Road until you reach the water.
